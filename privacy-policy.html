This Policy applies to our social media posting and management services across multiple platforms including LinkedIn, X (Twitter), TikTok, YouTube, Threads, Facebook, Instagram, and any other platforms we may support in the future. 2) Information We Collect

  • Social media account data you authorize via OAuth or API connections (e.g., user ID, name, profile information, channel/page information permitted by the scopes you grant, and access tokens) from platforms including:
    • LinkedIn (member ID, profile data, company pages)
    • X/Twitter (user ID, profile data, tweet permissions)

  • Operational logs and diagnostics (e.g., event timestamps, error logs, API response codes) to maintain and improve our services.

We do not intentionally collect sensitive categories of data unless you include such content in material you give us to post.

3) How We Use Information

  • To create, schedule, and publish content on your behalf across LinkedIn, X (Twitter), TikTok, YouTube, Threads, Facebook, Instagram, and other authorized platforms.
  • To provide support, troubleshoot issues, and improve reliability.
  • To comply with platform developer terms (LinkedIn Developer Agreement, X Developer Agreement, TikTok Developer Terms, YouTube API Services Terms, Meta Platform Terms for Facebook/Instagram/Threads), our service agreements, and applicable laws.

We may share data with service providers who help us operate our multi-platform services (e.g., cloud hosting, storage, logging, content delivery networks). These providers are bound by obligations to protect your data. We may also disclose information:

  • To the social media platforms (LinkedIn, X/Twitter, TikTok, YouTube, Threads, Facebook, Instagram) as necessary to publish your content through their APIs.

  • To protect our rights, property, or safety, or that of our users or the public.

6) Data Retention

  • Access tokens: kept only while valid or until you revoke access through the respective platform's settings.
  • Posts, videos, tweets, and schedules: retained as needed to provide the service or until you request deletion.
  • Platform-specific content: may be retained differently based on platform requirements and your service agreement.
  • Logs: typically retained up to 90 days for diagnostics and service improvement.

7) Security

We use encryption in transit (TLS/SSL), restricted access controls, secure secret storage, and platform-recommended security best practices. No method is 100% secure, but we continually improve protections and comply with each platform's security requirements.

8) Your Choices & Rights

  • Revoke access anytime through each platform's settings:
    • LinkedIn: Settings & Privacy → Data privacy → Other applications → Permitted services
    • X (Twitter): Settings and privacy → Security and account access → Apps and sessions → Connected apps
    • TikTok: Settings and privacy → Security → Manage apps
    • YouTube/Google: Google Account → Security → Third-party apps with account access
    • Threads: Profile → Settings → Account → Apps and websites
    • Facebook: Settings & Privacy → Settings → Security and Login → Apps and Websites
    • Instagram: Settings → Security → Apps and Websites (or manage through Facebook if accounts are linked)
  • Deletion requests: Email us to request deletion of data; we aim to respond within 30 days.
  • Access/Correction: Contact us to access, update, or correct your data.
  • Data portability: Request a copy of your data in a machine-readable format.
