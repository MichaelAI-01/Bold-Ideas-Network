Privacy Policy

Effective Date: August 17, 2025

Bold Ideas Network is an agency that manages and publishes posts on LinkedIn on behalf of the authorized account owner (the “Client”). This Privacy Policy describes what information we collect, how we use it, how we protect it, and the choices you have.

Contact: digitalworldaffiliate11@gmail.com

1) Scope

This Policy applies to our LinkedIn posting and management services and any related tools we provide. It does not cover third-party websites or services that we do not control.

2) Information We Collect

  • LinkedIn account data you authorize via LinkedIn OAuth (e.g., member ID, name, profile information permitted by the scopes you grant, and access tokens).
  • Content you provide (e.g., text, images, media, scheduling instructions) for posting on LinkedIn.
  • Page and post metadata (e.g., LinkedIn Page ID, post IDs/URLs, timestamps, status, limited performance metrics if available).
  • Operational logs and diagnostics (e.g., event timestamps, error logs) to maintain and improve our services.

We do not intentionally collect sensitive categories of data unless you include such content in material you give us to post.

3) How We Use Information

  • To create, schedule, and publish content on your behalf on LinkedIn.
  • To provide support, troubleshoot issues, and improve reliability.
  • To comply with LinkedIn’s developer terms, our agreements, and applicable laws.
  • To communicate with you about service updates and changes.

We do not sell your personal information.

4) Legal Bases (EEA/UK users)

  • Consent (Art. 6(1)(a)) when you authorize our app via LinkedIn OAuth.
  • Contract (Art. 6(1)(b)) to deliver services you request.
  • Legitimate interests (Art. 6(1)(f)) for security, diagnostics, and service improvement.

5) Sharing & Disclosures

We may share data with service providers who help us operate (e.g., cloud hosting, storage, logging). These providers are bound by obligations to protect your data. We may also disclose information if required by law.

6) Data Retention

  • Access tokens: kept only while valid or until you revoke access.
  • Posts and schedules: retained as needed to provide the service or until you request deletion.
  • Logs: typically retained up to 90 days for diagnostics.

7) Security

We use encryption in transit, restricted access, and secure secret storage. No method is 100% secure, but we continually improve protections.

8) Your Choices & Rights

  • Revoke access anytime from LinkedIn → Settings & Privacy → Data privacy → Other applications → Permitted services.
  • Deletion requests: Email us to request deletion of data; we aim to respond within 30 days.
  • Access/Correction: Contact us to update your data.

9) International Transfers

If data is transferred across borders, we rely on safeguards like standard contractual clauses as required by law.

10) Children’s Privacy

Our services are not intended for under-16s. We do not knowingly collect children’s data. Contact us if you believe we have and we will delete it.

11) Changes to this Policy

We may update this Policy. The “Effective Date” will change when updates are made. Material changes will be communicated.
